## Purpose
This project creates a **digital home for Coffee Cat Cafe**, a hidden café where cats are the main attraction. The platform aims to introduce and expand the café’s presence online, making it easier for visitors and feline enthusiasts to discover the café and its unique atmosphere.

Through the website, users can **meet the café’s adorable resident cats, explore their individual profiles, and browse a specialized menu**, all while experiencing the warm and relaxing environment that the café offers.

---

## Core Features

- **Interactive Cat Profile Gallery**  
  A collection of cat profiles showcasing each cat’s **personality, traits, and unique charm**.

- **Warm Aesthetic User Interface**  
  A carefully designed interface with **cozy visuals and welcoming colors** that reflect the relaxing atmosphere of the café.

- **Digital Café Menu**  
  An online menu featuring **dietary filters and seasonal specials**, allowing visitors to easily explore available food and drinks.

- **Café Introduction & History**  
  A short section that introduces **how the café started, its story, and the inspiration behind Coffee Cat Cafe**.
